About Sprunki

Welcome to Sprunki! Dive into this creative musical experience where you can mix sounds, create unique beats, and explore different character styles. Use your mouse to interact with the elements and build your own masterpiece.

Instructions:

  • Drag & Drop: Use your mouse to move items onto characters.
  • Mute/Solo: Click on characters to adjust their sound.
  • Bonus: Look for hidden combinations to unlock special animations!
